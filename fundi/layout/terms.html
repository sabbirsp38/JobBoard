COMPETITION RULES

dotFNB Nicolway promotion
Amended: 25 April 2012

By taking part in this competition, you confirm that you have read and understood the rules of this competition and agree to be legally bound to them (“the rules”).

The Competition Period:

The competition will start at 09h00 on 26 April 2012.
The competition will end at 19h00 on 10 May 2012 or once all the available prizes have been awarded.

How to Enter?
There are 2 ways to enter:

  • Visit FNB&rsquos;s new dotFNB store at the Nicolway Mall between 26 April and 10 May 2012 during the store&rsquos;s operating hours and “pop the dot” on the iPad in the store with the consultant, to see what prize you have won.
  • Download the Foursquare app onto your compatible smart phone* from the App store and register between 09h00 on 26 April 2012 and 19h00 on 10 May 2012. If you also visit (check in to) the new dotFNB store at the Nicolway Mall you will also stand in line to win a bigger prize. The more you check in (visit the store) the greater chance you have of becoming a mayor and winning the iPad 2. Note: You will need to visit our concept store in the Nicolway Mall to claim your prize.

Note: To register on Foursquare you must have one of these Smart phones: iPhone, Android, Blackberry, Nokia OVI and Palm. You are responsible for the cost of any equipment or services you need to take part in the competition, including data or calls.

Note: The prizes differ depending on how you enter. (See prizes below)

You can only win one prize even if you enter more than once.

Note: This competition is only being run at our new Nicolway store which is at Shop No. L31,Nicolway Mall, Corner William Nicol and Wedgewood Blink.

Who is allowed to take part in this competition?
Anybody may enter the promotion whether they are an FNB customer or not.
If you are not yet 18 you are only allowed to enter if your parent or legal guardian agrees to this. By entering you confirm that your parent or legal guardian consents to you taking part in his competition. You will be disqualified and will forfeit (give up) any prize if you lie about this.

The following persons may not take part in this competition: They will automatically be disqualified and will forfeit (give up) any prize:

  • Directors, members, employee, agents of, or consultants to FirstRand Bank Limited (the Bank) their promotional partners and printers, their advertising and their immediate families, including spouses, life partners, parents, children and siblings.
  • Any other person who directly or indirectly controls, or is controlled by the Bank.
  • Any supplier of goods and services in connection with this promotion.

If your name was chosen in the draw and you didn’t meet the criteria above or you were not allowed to enter, you will automatically be disqualified and another winner will be chosen in your place.

Prizes:
The prizes cannot be exchanged or redeemed for cash.

Persons who enter pop the dot at the dotFNB store stand a chance of winning ONE of the following prizes:

  • One of one iPad 2.
  • One of up to 80 x R 250 Nicolway shopping vouchers
  • One of up to 40 x R 500 Nicolway shopping vouchers
  • One of up to 900x 4 Gig memory sticks.

Persons who enter on Foursquare using their smart phone stand a chance of winning the following prizes depending on the category they fall into:


Category

 

Newbie (The first 100 people who register)

One of 100 4GIG memory sticks

Check in Specials

One of 40 R 250 Nicolway Shopping Vouchers

Mayor*

One of 1 iPad 2.

Mayorships are based on the most *days* with check-ins in while the competition is running (you could have more check-ins overall but fewer check-ins while the competition is running). Keep in mind that you might find yourself neck and neck with another user who is checking in to the venue often.  To be eligible, the setting "Participate in Foursquare Mayorships"must be checked and you must have a profile photo. Make sure you aren't checking in privately ("off the grid"). Off the grid check-ins do not count towards the Mayorship tally and will not unlock a Mayorship. When you check in, make sure to select Yes to "Share with friends?" and you should be able to unlock a crown. Only users who have legit check-ins (no rapid-fire check-ins, no drive-by check-ins, and no fake check-ins) will be eligible.

How are winners chosen?
If you entered at our dotmobi concept store: Your prize will immediately be selected by random draw during the pop in. If you entered using Foursquare: Your prize will be selected by means of a random draw. You will be notified on Foursquare. Note: You will need to visit our concept store in the Nicolway Mall to claim your prize. The customer who checks in most at the branch (is the Mayor) will win the iPad 2.

How will the winners be notified?
Winners will be notified immediately. Winners may be asked to take part in publicity for the promotion but they have the right to refuse.

How will we deliver the winners prize to them or how can a confirmed winner collect / claim their prize?
Winners who play in the branch and win the Nicolway shopping vouchers and 4 Gig memory sticks will receive their prize immediately. The iPad 2 will only be delivered to the winner within 72 hrs of FNB successfully confirming his or her identity. A copy of your Identification (Identity Document, passport or Drivers license) will be required for verification purposes. Note: Persons who enter via Foursquare need to visit the branch to claim their prize(s).

What happens if the promoter cannot contact the winner or if the winner is not able to take up the prize?
If the promoter cannot deliver the prize or if a winner doesn’t collect or claim the prize, within 30 (thirty) days after being notified of winning, he / she will lose the prize and another winner will be chosen. Proof of identity will be required when collecting a prize.
